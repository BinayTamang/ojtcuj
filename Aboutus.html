About Us

Fire Homestay at Darjelling, West Sikkim provides a gracious blend of warm hospitality and a beautiful stay. If you are looking for an ideal getaway with picturesque landscapes and a warm hospitality, here's a perfect place to drain out all your mental fatigue. Clean and simple Fire Home stay offers warmth of Sikkimese hospitality, home cooked authentic food and view of the Himalayas. Fire Homestay near Dalapchand, Darjelling, South Sikkim is an ideal place for a getaway into tranquility, a place to experience Sikkimese hospitality.

You can choose to visit many scenic and historic sightseeing places nearby like The Lampokari Lake, MANKHIM DARA, DAK BANGLOW, etc. If religion is the opening to your hearts and home, then this place is near to the holiest places of Darjelling like Chardham, Tarey Bhir, Saiddheshwar dham,Rose Garden Serdup Choling Monestry etc. Enjoy the historic and serpentine road at Zuluk, the famous Silk Route. And then, we will take care of your food and if you feel like indulging in some other authentic dishes of the region, or any other cuisines available, we are here to satisfy your taste buds.

When you visit us at Fire Homestay, you would be walking into a place with a personal touch to everything and unassuming hospitality. People usually visit us to hike – enjoy nature up, close and personal, or to get away from the hectic city life. Look out for a beautiful view of hills, sunrise, sunset and also enjoy the rejuvenating walk around Lampokhari Lake at Darjelling.
